Unique Gameplay Mechanics

Unlike traditional Tic-Tac-Toe where marks stay permanently, Infinite Tic-Tac-Toe introduces a dynamic twist - each player can only have 3 marks on the board at any time. When placing a fourth mark, your oldest mark disappears, creating an ever-evolving strategic battlefield!
